Our story

🌸 食

Cooked with
roots and
curiosity.

Halo Halo House was born between two friends — one from Cebu, one from Busan — bonding over their grandmothers' kitchens and a shared love of big flavours in small plates.

Every dish tells a double story: the comfort of home, and the thrill of discovery. Come hungry, leave with a new favourite.
